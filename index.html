Services

Our intersciplinary collaborations lead to varied services focused on bringing cutting-edge research in Sciences, Engineering, Tech, & Math to your personal and professional endeavors.

Digital Privacy

We offer education and training in data privacy for your personal and professional data. Our digital security is only as important as the costs of compromise which are fortunately minor with modern banking identity theft protection, 2FA (2-Factor Autentication), and HTTPS/SSL (Secure Internet Browsing). However, understanding our digital self can prevent headaches and annoying conversations with automated customer service representatives. Take control of your Digital-Self to empower yourself in the Digital Age.

Social Media

Finding ourselves was hard enough before the Internet. Now, our personalities are split between countless social media platforms which can stress us out and often leads to poor well-being. We offer social media consulting to align your personality with your online presence so you can leverage your social media accounts as a brand of individuality rather than the vehicle that loses your Digital-Self.

Applications

Central to our services, our Applications Development Program works to put cutting-edge research into your pocket. We work to accelerate the life cycle of discovery-to-product from the academic world by connecting developers and scientists, bringing ideas into the market polished and accessible to everyone. Check out our project section to learn more about our active projects or Contact Us to find or submit projects you are interested in.

About Us

Our mission is to translate the world's information into accessible and useful knowledge.

Pseudostem is an information and technology organization dedicated to integrating discoveries in research into our everyday lives.

We build solutions and offer services to help the world filter and capitalize on the staggering amount of information imparted on our daily lives.

We connect researchers and industry, scientists and communities, students and entrepreneurs in an effort to learn from one another and build a better future.
