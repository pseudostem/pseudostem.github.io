Digital Privacy

We offer education and training in data privacy for your personal and professional data. Our digital security is only as important as the costs of compromise which are fortunately minor with modern banking identity theft protection, 2FA (2-Factor Autentication), and HTTPS/SSL (Secure Internet Browsing). However, understanding our digital self can prevent headaches and annoying conversations with automated customer service representatives. Take control of your Digital Identity to empower yourself in the Digital Age.

Social Media

Finding ourselves was hard enough before the Internet. Now, our personalities are split between countless social media platforms which can stress us out and often leads to poor well-being. We offer Social Media Consulting to align your personality with your online presence so you can leverage your social media accounts as a brand of individuality rather than the vehicle that loses y our Digital-Self.
